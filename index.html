Projects

Healthy Future. Health Troy.

First announced in 2012 by St. Peter’s Health Partners, the Troy Master Facility Plan was a $99.9 million, multi-phased plan to transform healthcare in Rensselaer County and its surrounding communities.

The project included construction, renovation, and modernization efforts aimed at improving inpatient facilities on the Samaritan campus and outpatient facilities on the St. Mary’s campus.

As Communications and Marketing Manager, I led the marketing and communications efforts throughout all phases of the project, including plan development and implementation.

Working with a local creative firm, an overarching campaign theme - Healthy Future. Healthy Troy. - was developed. This theme and consistent visual concept was woven into all campaign communications and marketing materials throughout the six-year project.

Major elements of the campaign included:

  • A unique website landing page and web pages providing project updates, construction alerts, and milestones
  • Colleague e-newsletter
  • Print advertising
  • Digital ads
  • Social media
  • Outdoor (Out of Home)
  • Pull-up displays
  • Collateral materials
  • Direct mail
  • Television ads
  • Signage
  • Earned media

Take a Stand Troy

A sexual Assualt Bystander Campaign

The primary target audience for the campaign was adults age 18-34 in Troy, NY. The grant-funded campaign was designed to encourage individuals witnessing harassment or abuse to step forward and take action rather than just being a bystander. This initiative is coordinated through the Sexual Assault and Crime Victims Assistance Program.

The creative for the campaign required compelling photography and featured well-known individuals in the Troy community. The campaign elements included:

  • Website
  • Social Media
  • Press Release/ Press Event
  • Poster Series
  • Panel Cards
  • Car Window Clings
